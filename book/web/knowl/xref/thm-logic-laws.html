Theorem 1.4.6. Logic Laws.

  • \(\displaystyle \sim (\sim P)\equiv P\)
Double negation Law
  • \(\displaystyle P\wedge Q\equiv Q\wedge P\)
  • \(\displaystyle P\vee Q\equiv Q\vee P\)
Commutativity Laws
  • \(\displaystyle P\wedge (Q\vee R)\equiv (P\wedge Q)\vee (P\wedge R)\)
  • \(\displaystyle P\vee (Q\wedge R)\equiv (P\vee Q)\wedge (P\vee R)\)
Distributivity Laws
  • \(\displaystyle P\vee P\equiv P\)
  • \(\displaystyle P\wedge P\equiv P\)
Absorption Laws
  • \(\displaystyle \sim (P\vee Q)\equiv (\sim P)\wedge (\sim Q)\)
  • \(\displaystyle \sim (P\wedge Q)\equiv (\sim P)\vee (\sim Q)\)
DeMorgan’s Law
  • \(\displaystyle (P\vee Q)\vee R\equiv P\vee (Q\vee R)\)
  • \(\displaystyle (P\wedge Q)\wedge R\equiv P\wedge (Q\wedge R)\)
Associativity Laws
  • \(\displaystyle [P\implies Q]\equiv[(\sim Q)\implies(\sim P)]\)
Contrapositive
  • \(\displaystyle P\implies Q\equiv (\sim P)\vee Q\)
