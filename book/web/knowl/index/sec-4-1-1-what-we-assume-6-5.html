Item 5.

The Division Algorithm
\(\forall a,b\in\Z\text{,}\) with \(a\neq0\text{,}\) \(\exists! q,r\in\Z\) such that
\begin{equation*} b=aq+r \end{equation*}
where \(0\leq r\lt |a|\text{.}\) The \(q\) is called the quotient and the \(r\) is called the remainder.
