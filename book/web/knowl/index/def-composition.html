Definition 7.2.4. Composite.

Let \(R\) be a relation from \(A\) to \(B\text{,}\) and let \(S\) be a relation from \(B\) to \(C\text{.}\) The composite of \(R\) and \(S\) is
\begin{equation*} S\circ R = \{(a,c)\text{ } |\text{ } \exists b \in B \text{ such that } (a,b)\in R \text{ and } (b,c) \in S\} \end{equation*}
