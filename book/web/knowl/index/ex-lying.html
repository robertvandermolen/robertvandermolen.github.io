Example 1.2.2. The Lying Politician.

\(P\text{:}\) I am elected
\(Q\text{:}\) I will lower taxes
\(P\implies Q\text{:}\) If I am elected then I will lower taxes.
So when is politician lying?
There are a couple of cases:
  1. The politician is indeed elected:
    1. The politician lowers taxes!
      The politiciian is not lying!
    2. The politician does not lower taxes
      The politician is lying!
  2. The politician is not elected:
    1. The politician works hard with community leaders and lobbies to get taxes lowered
      The politician is not lying!
    2. The politician sits on their couch all day eating Fruit Loops, and taxes are not lowered
      The politician is not lying!
