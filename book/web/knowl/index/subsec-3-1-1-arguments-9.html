Example 2.1.4.

Here are some example of valid arguments:
All parabolas are functions of degree 2
All functions of degree 2 are quadratic
\(\therefore \) All parabolas are quadratic
An apple is purple
An apple is a fruit
\(\therefore \) An apple is purple
All spiders have 8 legs
A poodle is a spider
\(\therefore \) A poodle has 8 legs
Here is an example of an invalid argument:
All chickens are animals that eat corn
All chickens are animals that have wings
\(\therefore \) All animals that eat corn have wings
