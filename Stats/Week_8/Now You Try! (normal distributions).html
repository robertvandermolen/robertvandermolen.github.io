Normal Distributions

Now You Try!

1) The average daily jail population in the United States is 706,242. If the distribution is normal and the standard deviation is 52,145, find the probability that on a randomly selected day, the jail population is

  • Greater than 75,000
  • Between 6000,000 and 700,000

2) The national average SAT score (for Verbal and Math) is 1028. If we assume a normal distribution with \(\sigma= 92\), what is the 90th percentile score? What is the probability that a randomly selected score exceeds 1200?

3) The average number of calories in a 1.5-ounce chocolate bar is 225. Suppose that the distribution of calories is approximately normal with \(\sigma = 10\). Find the probability that a randomly selected chocolate bar will have

  • Between 200 and 220 calories
  • Less than 200 calories
